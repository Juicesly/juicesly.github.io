The Collective

Unity Game · 3D · Systems Design · Collaboration

The Collective is a collaborative 3D game project focused on coordinated systems, spatial design, and player interaction. The project emphasizes communication between mechanics, level structure, and audiovisual feedback, developed through a shared production pipeline.
